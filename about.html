The Curator

Bakery Thrift was born out of a deep appreciation for the silhouettes and stories of the 90s. Every piece you see here has been hand-selected, cleaned, and preserved to ensure it finds a second life in your wardrobe.

I believe that vintage clothing is more than just a trend—it’s an archive of culture. My goal is to provide a curated selection of genuine, era-specific finds that look and feel just as good today as they did thirty years ago.

Thank you for choosing to shop small and support the preservation of vintage style.
